When should I RSVP by?

Please RSVP via this website by August 6, 2022

Can I Bring a Guest?

Everyone invited will be listed on the invitation and on the RSVP website function. If we can accommodate a guest, they will either have their name listed or your invitation will say your name "and Guest".

What time should I arrive for the ceremony?

We would love for you to arrive 10-15 minutes early for the ceremony to provide ample time to park and find a seat.

Is there parking for the ceremony and reception?

Yes, both the ceremony and reception are at Silverwood Great Hall in St. Anthony and there is ample complimentary parking in their parking lot.

Are there hotels nearby?

Yes, there are. If you go to the menu where you found this page, you can navigate to the Travel tab and find hotel information there. We have blocks reserved at three different hotels within 10 minutes of Silverwood Great Hall where you can reserve rooms for a group rate.

Is the venue accessible?

Yes, everything in on one level and there are accessible restrooms.

I have dietary restrictions, what can I eat?

We are having two entrees served in a buffet: pulled pork and lasagna. The pulled pork is gluten-free, and the lasagna is vegetarian. Beyond that, we will have signs noting what is dietary restriction friendly.

What is the dress code?

We would love to see you in semiformal attire. Not sure what that looks like? Here's a link to some ideas: Semiformal Dress Examples

Are either the ceremony or reception going to be held outside?

Nope! Both the ceremony and reception are being held inside Silverwood Great Hall. The hall does have an outdoor patio that can be utilized during the reception and cocktail hour if you want some fresh air.
