The Minnesota State Fair

Labor Day Weekend is the last weekend of the nationally ranked Minnesota State Fair. If you are interested in prizewinning livestock, deliciously greasy cheesecurds, or famous Sweet Martha's Cookies, we recommend you take a day to explore the fair while you're in town.

Minnehaha Falls

Minnehaha Falls not only has a lovely waterfall, but is also right next to the delicious restaurant Sea Salt.

Minnesota Landscape Arboretum

At the arboretum, you can walk through gorgeous floral gardens and other natural spaces, or go on the Three-Mile Drive to view various collections and landscapes. Reservations Required.

Bde Maka Ska

One of the many lakes in MN with lots of activities. Walking and biking paths are available, along with picnic areas and various rental opportunities including canoes, kayaks, and paddleboards.
