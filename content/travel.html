We have reserved blocks of rooms at 3 hotels within 10 minutes of Silverwood Great Hall. The rooms are booked from Thursday, September 1, 2022 to Sunday, September 4, 2022. You are, of course, not obligated to book at these hotels, though you will receive the group rate if you do.

To book rooms at any of these hotels, you may call them and request a room in the Trahan/Swift Wedding Block, or go through the provided links.

Please reserve your desired room by Tuesday, August 2, 2022 as any unbooked rooms will be released to the public after that. The group rate will still apply after this date, pending room availability.

September 1-4 is the last weekend of the MN State Fair, as well as Labor Day Weekend. If you do not book by August 2, hotel availability is likely to be limited.
