The Minnesota State Fair

Labor Day Weekend is the last weekend of the nationally ranked Minnesota State Fair. If you are interested in big livestock, cheesecurds, or famous Sweet Martha's Cookies, we recommend you take a day to explore the fair while you're in town.

Minnehaha Falls
